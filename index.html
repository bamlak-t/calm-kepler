How to Play

Seekers

Goal: Navigate your pieces to deduce and occupy the hidden Mastermind tile.

  • Movement: Your pieces move like Chess pieces. Click a piece, then click a valid destination.
  • Clues: Landing on a new tile reveals a clue eliminating possibilities (e.g. "Not on a Red tile").
  • Accusation: When you are confident, move a piece onto the suspected tile, select it, and click "Accuse Selection". A wrong accusation loses the game!

Cipher Duel

Goal: Deduce your opponent's 4-digit code (1-9) before they deduce yours.

  • Inspection: Click a symbol on the grid to reveal a constraint about the opponent's code.
  • Grid Logic: The row determines which digit the clue targets. The symbol determines the type of clue (Parity, Difference, Exact Value, etc).
  • Burned Tiles: Inspecting a tile consumes it for both players, but only the inspector sees the clue.
  • End Turn: After inspecting, click End Turn. Or, click Crack Code to try and win!
