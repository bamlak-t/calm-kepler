📖 Game Rules & Instructions

Select a game below to see exactly how to play its current prototype version:

👥 2-Player Pass-and-Play (Hotseat)

All games in this lab are built as local 2-player pass-and-play games on the same screen. To support this:

  • When your turn ends, the screen will blank with a "Pass the Device" screen.
  • Hand the device/mouse to your partner before clicking "I'm Ready". This prevents you from seeing each other's secret information!

🕵️ Funny Sleuths

Goal: Find the coordinates of the hidden mastermind tile first.

  • Each player gets 3 investigators. Their movements are randomized each game (e.g., Scout moves like a Knight, Analyst like a Rook).
  • Click your piece on your turn, then click a highlighted destination tile to move.
  • Stepping on a new tile reveals a clue in the log (e.g., "Target row is even" or "Manhattan distance is 3").
  • Combine clues to narrow down the target tile. Click "Make Accusation", select the target tile, and submit to check if you are right. Wrong accusation loses!

⚖️ Rules of Evidence

Goal: Match every suspect to their correct Weapon, Room, and Motive.

  • A secret matching is generated at start.
  • Spend your Inquiry Points to test custom pairings (e.g., "Is Beatrix related to Poison?").
  • Click on the grid's cells to track your deductions manually (turns cell to ✓, ✗, or ?).
  • When you think you've solved it, click "Submit Solution". Match all 3 suspects correctly to win. An incorrect solution gives the win to your opponent!

🔐 Cipher Duel

Goal: Crack your opponent's secret 4-digit code (numbers 1–9) before they crack yours.

  • Setup Phase: Each player enters a secret 4-digit code in secret.
  • Play Phase: Take turns clicking on symbols on the 5×5 grid. Each symbol gives a mathematical constraint on the opponent's code.
  • Constraints accumulate in the sidebar. Revealed digits automatically show up in your slot tracker.
  • Click "Crack the Code", enter your 4-digit guess, and submit to win.

👤 Whispers in the Dark

Goal: As the Fugitive, reach the 🚪 Exit node. As the Detective, catch the Fugitive.

  • Fugitive (Player 1): Moves invisibly through node connections. You leave a trail of "noise" which decays over turns.
  • Detective (Player 2): Moves visibly. When you land on a node, you get a noise readout (Very Hot, Warm, Cool, Cold) indicating how recently the Fugitive was there.
  • Use the bottom action buttons to select your move each turn. Fugitive escapes to win; Detective catches Fugitive to win.
